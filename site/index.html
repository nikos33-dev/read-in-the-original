How to use

Pick a passage from the list on the left.

Hover any word to see what it means — the meaning that fits this line shows first, with the word's other senses below.

Click any word to highlight its phrase and read a grammar explanation of just that phrase, plus a translation of the whole sentence.

On accuracy: the per-word hover glosses are dictionary best-guesses and can be off for archaic or poetic usage. The grammar explanation and full-sentence translation are produced by reading the whole line in context, so they're more reliable — trust them when a single word's gloss looks wrong.
